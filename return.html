Refund and Returns Policy

At Bibsol, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, here's what you need to know:

1. Timeframe

Our refund and returns policy lasts for 30 days from the date of purchase. Unfortunately, if more than 30 days have passed, we won't be able to offer you a full refund or exchange.

2. Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused and in its original packaging.

3. Exceptions

Please note that certain items cannot be returned, such as perishable goods (like food, flowers, newspapers, or magazines), intimate or sanitary goods, hazardous materials, or flammable liquids or gases. Additionally, gift cards, downloadable software products, and some health and personal care items are non-returnable.

4. Proof of Purchase

To complete your return, we'll need a receipt or proof of purchase. Please don't send the item back to the manufacturer.

5. Partial Refunds

Here are a few situations where only partial refunds may be granted:

  • If a book shows obvious signs of use.
  • If a CD, DVD, VHS tape, software, video game, cassette tape, or vinyl record has been opened.
  • If an item is not in its original condition, is damaged, or is missing parts for reasons not due to our error.
  • If an item is returned more than 30 days after delivery.

6. Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed once we receive and inspect your returned item. We'll send you an email to notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be applied to your original method of payment within a certain amount of days.

7. Refund Delays

If you haven't received your refund yet, please check your bank account and contact your credit card company. It may take some time for the refund to be officially posted.
